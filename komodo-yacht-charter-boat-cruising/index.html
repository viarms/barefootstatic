Komodo Yacht Charter: Cruising in the Land of Dragons

A Komodo Yacht Charter is one of the most sought-after Highlights of Cruising  Indonesia. Home of the most amazing sceneries for photographers, scuba divers, hikers, beach strollers. Visit the last dragons on earth in a land that time forgot. A private boat charter with Barefoot Yachts with give you the freedom to personalize the itineraries and activities according to your preferences. Our Komodo Charters are tailored to fit the style, budget and timeframe of your group and are suitable for Adults and Kids alike.

A traditional Phinisi Schooner is a comfortable yet adventurous way to travel to Komodo National Park and beyond.  Relax on the deck, be with friends and family, or experience one of the many land and water-based activities Komodo has to offer – Scuba Diving, Hiking, Snorkeling or Sea Kayaking. Climb a breathtaking viewpoint for sunset, stroll along a virgin beach or observe huge flying bats chasing the night while the sun gives way to a blanket of stars.  

Komodo Highlights

Komodo Dragons

Meet the last dragons on earth, the Komodo Dragons, while cruising around Komodo National Park. The park was founded in 1980 in order to preserve its fragile habitat, only found in this tiny corner of the world. These dragons can be found in Rinca as well as Komodo island, along with various other wildlife. Choose to visit one of the locations or both.

Manta Rays

Manta Rays Taka Makassar is one of the most popular site for witnessing large aggregations of Manta Rays. Very often you can also see them close by  in and around sites such as Mawan or further south in Manta Alley.

Secret Beaches

Each boat and Cruise Director has some own secret beaches around the Komodo National Park. Let the Crew aboard the Yacht guide you to lesser known wonders away from the pressure of mainstream tourism.

Padar

Padar is a grassland covered picturesque landscape connected by 3 blue bays. Each bay features unique beaches, one is a sparkling white sanded beach, another is a very unique blush pink and the last is a black beach, getting its' charcoal hues from volcanic origin.

Kalong Island

The Flying Foxes, a varied species of a fruit bat, of Kalong Island ascend at dusk for their nightly hunt. The setting is truly unique with thousands of them rising into the sky during the orange and red glow of a Komodo Sunset.

Advanced Diving

Komodo is a thrill for Advanced Divers. Sites like Batu Bolong, Castle Rock and Shotgun are packed with life and can offer some of the best Diving in the world. Cruising from one dive site to the next gives you a chance to stretch your diving skills and rediscover some of the most varied undersea flora and fauna.

Beginner Diving

Although Komodo is well known for several advanced diving sites, there are many protected sites for kids and beginners. Sebayur, Kanawa, Siaba Besar, Bidadari are just some of them. Komodo is a wonderful place to get your feet wet and explore the world of diving.

Gili Lawa Darat

The area between Gili Lawa Darat and Gili Lawa Laut is a excellent place to spend an entire day on a Yacht and visiting surrounding sites. There are many dive and snorkel sites as well as a iconic viewpoint. This is one of the many areas perfectly suited for photography.

Pink Beach

Pink Beach is iconic for the soft pink color of its' sand. The soft sand gets its blushed hue from crushed red corals pushed on land by soft crystal clear tides. It is a great stop on your cruise for a quiet stroll, beautiful photo opportunities and a relaxed snorkel.

Horseshoe Bay

Meet the last dragons on earth, the Komodo Dragons, while cruising around Komodo National Park. The park was founded in 1980 in order to preserve its fragile habitat, only found in this tiny corner of the world.  These dragons can be found in Rinca as well as Komodo island, along with various other wildlife. Choose to visit one of the locations or both.

Banta Island

Banta Island is just off to the north west off the Komodo National Park. Currents are generally strong here as a result of it being one of the largest gaps between islands in Nusa Tenggara. You can explore Dive Sites such as GPS Point, Tanjung Rasa, Star Wars and Galley Rock. This is a great spot to integrate when choosing which activities to do on a longer Komodo Yacht Charter around Komodo or Cruising towards Lombok.

Kanawa

On the small beautiful tropical island of Kanawa, you can relax in the luxury of nature. The White Sand Beach is great for kids and adults alike and there are some easy, beginner level dive and snorkelling sites in the area.

Mesa Village

Mingle with the lovely village Locals and visit the descendants of the of sea gypsies on Mesa. Meet the curious local children and catch a glimpse of the islanders' daily lives - drying sea cucumbers and processing shell fish, amongst other things. Mesa" means "itchy" and the island was named so due to the itchy sand.

Siaba Besar

This is another amazing dive and Snorkelling site. Siaba Besar has a beautiful coral garden and amazing life under the surface. This dive site can be dived in rising and falling tide, depending on where you enter. This gorgeous bay is a great spot to see often barracudas, trevally and black tip sharks!

Sebayur

Popular for limestone formations, Sebayur is a perfect location for relaxed Diving and Snorkelling or some Sea Kayaking. This a great location to take in the vivid blues and greens of Komodo.

Komodo Boat Charters

The most popular highlight of a Komodo Boat Charter are the famous Dragons on Rinca or Komodo Island – the only places in the world to meet this prehistoric predator. Next to reptile inhabitants, the islands around Komodo offer abundant Marine sites for Diving and Snorkeling. Large Pelagic Fish encounters as well a Dugongs and Whale sightings are common.
Most remarkably, encounter Manta Rays on nearly all of our Komodo Yacht Charters. If you are keen on land-based activities you will not fall short. Experience Komodo’s most scenic peak views and enjoy the panoramic views over the Island Archipelago. The island of Padar is the most popular view point, but there are many more impressive peaks to climb.

Spend a night stargazing on the deck of your private yacht. No pollution makes the Milky Way sparkle and appear in its full beauty. Many of our Boat  also offer stand up paddleboards and sea kayaks – perfect Toys for Adults and the Kids onboard. Motorized watersports such as Jet Skiing, Waterskiing and Game Fishing are available outside the National Park area. See here for the top Highlights of Komodo Sailing.

Komodo Good To Know

Where does the Yacht depart?

Labuan Bajo is the gateway for Yacht Charters in Komodo. Labuan Bajo is reachable by domestic flights from Bali, Jakarta and Makassar.

What to bring to a Komodo Yacht Charter

  • Sun Protection (Head/glasses/Cream)
  • Pocket Cash for, Crew Tips, Souvenirs and National Park Fees
  • Light Clothing, Swim Wear, Sweater,Camera.
  • Towels/Basic Toiletries are provided, but it’s not a bad idea to bring some own.
  • Snorkelling/Diving Gear – we have gear, but personal gear always fits best.

What is the season for Charters in Komodo?

Generally, Yacht Charters in Komodo are possible all year round. The most popular season is June - September and January - March is considered low season. May and April and October and November are excellent month for a boat charters in Flores.
