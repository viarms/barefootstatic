SURFING CHARTERS MALUKU ISLANDS

Our Surfing Charters around Maluku Islands brings you to the remotest world-class surf breaks in Indonesia. This is the ultimate chance to discover virgin waves of the North Indonesian Pacific Ocean. The Cruise around Morotai in the Malukas for a true surfing adventure around remote islands. Around 600 islands that combine the area in here a huge potential for countless surf breaks. Besides, they offer a feast of beautiful bays, rugged coastlines and serene tropical solitude. Spectacular volcanoes, lush green jungle and crystal clear waters invite you to embark on our Surf Charter Cruise. Enjoy private surf breaks, away from the often crowed waves of Bali or Lombok. Cruise the islands of North Maluku from the privacy and comfort of a traditional Phinisi boat.

Surfing NOrthern Maluku

The North Maluku Islands are scattered in the North Eastern edge of Indonesia between Sulawesi and Papua. They face the open sea of the Pacific Ocean. Thus the monsoon season provides consistent surf from November until March. Additionally, September and October may even grant us with some typhoon swells generated up North in the Philippines. Nevertheless, winds are generally light and you can expect glassy days during this time.

Private line up waiting for the next set of waves.

Custom Made Surf Charters IndoNesia

Cruising along these beautiful shorelines with private Surf Charter Indonesia will encounter a great variety of perfect waves. Our local crew will know the best bays to hit in any conditions. Your Surf Cruise Indonesia will usually start and finish in Tobelo island. The easiest way is to fly from Bali or Jakarta to the airport of Kao Tobelo city – we are happy to help arranging your flights.

Having waves privately is common around Halmahera.

World Class Surf Breaks of Morotai

Here is just a brief idea of our top four picks in the area:

Serenade: Left-hander that wraps around the reef. Make the ledgy take-off and tuck into the fast barrel before you smoothly roundhouse the second section.

Sidewalk: Mellow small day option for easy, but long rides. Great option for longboarders, too.

Indo Jiwa: Epic right-hander with hollow walls and a fast outside section. Access by land is very hard, so expect so be alone with your mates.

Short Ledge: A good right, thick, high tide barrel on the side of a stunning bay.

The exact route will always be adjusted according to wind and tides to hit the waves at the best possible time.

Raja AmPat & Moluccas Adventure

The trip starts in the heart of Raja Ampat (Waisai) and as it’s the epicenter of marine biodiversity in the world its a perfect location to wash of the travel right of the bat to set the tone. The first stop and initial heading of the trip will be determined by swell forecast. Either it entails an overnight to get to a place that holds 4 different waves in close proximity with levels from gentle rides to slabby barrels. The other route would start with snorkeling and diving for the first day or two before getting to an area with wave selection. Mid trip the variety of spots hold both waves and stunning exploring with not only diving/snorkeling but hikes/climbs, cliff jumps, authentic Papuan village experiences and an endless opportunity of finding new locations. The pacific pulses swell in a shorter period with quickly changing conditions and knowing where to be at what time is essential. It is not your average surf trip with ticking boxes but it’s a true last frontier trip with having the explorers hat on. Line ups will be empty except the odd local kid on a piece of wood. 

Morotai Surfing Trips

Mortai Surfing Trips bring you in the last surfing frontier in Indonesia and offer en experience very different compared to Bali and many other surfing destinations. The main islands of Halmahera and Morotai offer plenty of known waves waves and the opportunity to explore and  find new once… Surfing tris are not exclusively about surfing but offer a chance to explore one of the remotest regions of the world and send your time fishing, making beach bbqs and visiting local villages.

Best Season Surfing Maluku

The best surfing season around Maluku and Mortai is from November to March during the South Pacific cyclone seasons. In this time wind is often mild and the surf solid.
