Tiger Blue

  • 34 M
  • 5 CabinS
  • 14 Crew

Tiger Blue is a remarkable 34-meter traditional Indonesian sailing yacht. Built in 2008 and completely refurbished in 2018, she is designed with adventure and comfort in mind. The attention to details shows the knowledge and skills of the traditional craftsmanship she was constructed with. Her 5 ensuite cabins can accommodate up to 12 guests and the open spaces onboard Tiger Blue provide enough space for tranquil moments of bliss and soaking up some sun. Dedicated service and delicious meals complement your journey on this beauty and make her the ideal choice for your private boat charter.

Cruise on Tiger Blue

Tiger Blue has been cruising the Indonesian Archipelago for more than a decade and this experience is reflected in the world-class service. As her name implies, hues of blue are perfectly complementing the natural teak and ironwood. The impressive open-living layout allows for maximum comfort and immersion into the beautiful landscape. Her main deck offers a large alfresco dining table, seating area and two double day beds. Tiger Blue’s crew of 10 includes an experienced captain and accomplished cruise director. The head chef and the rest of the crew are professionals and will ensure your comfort and safety at any time.

Cabins

Tiger Blue’s 5 ensuite cabins can host up to 12 guests. On the upper deck, there is a double cabin with easy access to the yacht’s dining and seating area. The cabin features a king size bed and large windows that allow for natural light in the cabin. The 4 remaining cabins are located on the lower deck. The two double cabins and the two twin cabins, which can set up as triple on request, feature portholes and a smart layout. All cabins onboard Tiger Blue are equipped with air conditioning, fresh linens, complimentary toiletries, and hot water showers.

Remote Islands

Exploring remote islands onboard Tiger Blue is an experience not to miss. From the unique land and sea scape of Komodo National Park to the innumerable limestone island clusters of Raja Ampat, Tiger Blue is expertly guiding guests to the far reaches of the diverse Indonesian Archipelago. Fully equipped for diving and snorkelling the yacht has much to offer. Kayaks, wakeboards, and water skis are readily available for guests. The range of actives on board and off board ensures that everyone has an unforgettable journey.
