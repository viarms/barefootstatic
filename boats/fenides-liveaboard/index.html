Fenides Liveaboard

  • 41M
  • 5 CabinS
  • 11 Crew

Say hello to Fenides Liveaboard, your 41-meter luxury phinisi yacht and guide to unrivaled adventures in the Indonesian Archipelago. Built in 2019 by the masterful Konjo boat builders of Ara,  she is expertly designed and showcases impeccable attention to detail. Welcoming up to 11 guests in her 5 luxury cabins, Fenides and her crew are devoted to the highest level of service, safety and satisfaction. She features a main deck, upper deck and a lower deck. All decks are spacious with enough space for relaxation and onboard activities. Enjoy the spectacular views of the Indonesian landscape and restore your energy with a fresh juice. Fenides takes you to the most beautiful spots in Komodo National Park and Raja Ampat. Special surf charters to Mortai and Halmahera are available on request. 

About Fenides

Fenides was designed by an esteemed architect and constructed by the legendary Konjo boat builders of Ara. She highlights the symbiotic relationship between traditional Indonesian boat building and the modern luxury cruising experience. Constructed from tropical Ironwood and native Teak wood, Fenides redefines luxury diving liveaboard standards. With accents of modern art, bright colors and natural wood furniture she creates a warm and serene atmosphere. The large indoor spaces and wide-open decks are perfectly arranged. The fully equipped camera room for editing and video engineering is another highlight onboard.

Fenides Cabins

The concept of luxury and comfort continues in her 5 cabins which can accommodate up to 11 guests. She features 1 master cabin and two twin cabins on the lower deck and two double cabins on the upper deck. All cabins are equipped with en-suit bathrooms, air conditioning, HD TVs and media systems. The warm and light colors complement the delightful experience onboard Fenides.

Cruise Experience

Allow yourself to lean back and enjoy a cruise experience like no other. With an exciting range of water sports, leisure activities and premium dining, Fenides spoils her guests in an unparalleled way. When diving with Fenides, expect to explore underwater life in the most exciting way. Whether you are muck diving, drift diving or night diving, Fenides will guide you to world class dive sites. Cruising on Fenides doesn’t stop at diving—opt for a surf charter and venture to Indonesia’s most famous barrels and some top-secret wave breaks on unknown islands.

Technical

  • Beam 7.70m
  • Draft 2.00m
  • Generators: 2x Yanmar 33kva
  • Engine: Yanmar 6HA2M WDT (298kw/405hp)
  • Communication:VHF, SSB, Satellite
  • Cruising speed: 9 knots
  • Navigation:Garmin GPS, Garmin Radar
  • Fuel Capacity 6.000 litres
  • Freshwater Capacity 6.000 litres
Tender Boats

2 Tenders, 50 HP Engines (Yamaha)

Dining Options

Al fresco dining onboard is sure to please all of the senses as the onboard chef serves a range of western and Indonesian fine cuisine. Choose to have your meal surrounded by incredible views on the open-air deck or enjoy dining ashore a deserted white sand beach under the stars.
