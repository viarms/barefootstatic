Phinisi Liveaboard Duyung Baru

  • 27 m
  • 3 CabinS
  • 5 Crew

The 27-meter, traditional Indonesian Phinisi liveaboard Duyung Baru provides an affordable luxury liveaboard experience around Komodo and Sulawesi. The 3 cabins on board cater to a maximum of 6 guests. The boat offers a personalized and intimate cruising experience and makes her the ideal Family Boat Charter and perfect for Komodo diving cruises. Duyung Baru, the New Mermaid, is also an excellent choice for Komodo freediving trips. She is constructed in an extremely practical, clean and efficient way. The experience of the owner is displayed in every single detail and shown in the flawless layout.

The Phinisi

Duyung Baru or ‘The New Mermaid’ is a gorgeous schooner. Built in 2010 on the shore of Bira and completely renovated in 2016. The traditional Phinisi vessel is set-up specifically with diving and cruising in mind. Do not think that being ‘traditional’ means lack of safety or luxury. Duyung Baru is equipped with high-tech safety systems and designed for a unique cruising experience of comfort and adventure. .

Cruising

During the cruise, a top deck lounge provides enough space for sunbathing, relaxing or simply enjoying the views. The Phinisi liveaboard has a beautiful kitchen and dining area with an open space. The food on board is prepared with love. The selection of dishes ranges from fresh croissants and bread for breakfast to an delicious fusion of traditional Asian and Western foods. The owner Yani is onboard herself and ensures the highest quality of service.

Cabins

Guests are accommodated in two private double cabins and one twin cabin. The tastefully designed and spacious luxury cabins have all air conditioning and hot showers. Double cabins are equipped with a king size bed and spring mattresses. Single beds are 2m X 1.40m in size and also have spring mattresses. Enjoy your adventure in comfort and well rested.

Duyung Baru Specifications

Technical

Overall Length: 27 m
Beam: 6.0 m
Draft: 1,80 m
Sailing Area: 260 m2
 2-Mast Phinisi Rig
Diesel Tanks: 3 ton
Main marine Engine: YANMAR marine, 6 Cylinder Diesel
Cruising Speed: 8 knots max 10 knots
Solar panels: 4 SOLAR
2 Generators: 12KW, 9KW 3 Phase
Tender : 5,60 Meter, 
Navigation Equipment: GPS – Furuno, fish finder – Garmin, radio – Icom

Dive & Leisure equipment
  • 8 Sets Diving Equipment
  • Kids diving equipment
  • kids Tanks & 15L Tanks
  • 2 x Bauer Compressor/Nitrox available
  • 1 Tandem Kanu
  • BBQ onboard for fresh fish 
Tender Boat
6m Aluminum Tender 2 x 80Hp – fast and reliable transpotation for diving and snorkelling.
Cabins

All cabins are are fully air-conditioned 24h and feature a separate shower and toilet area. Shower Gel, Shampoo and seperate shower/beach towels will be provided.

Water-maker & Electricity
  • 24hour  Electricity and wifi according to area.
  • Quality fresh water is available throughout the entire Yacht. (1500l/day)
Food

 Great breakfast including fresh bread, croissants, cake and traditional indonesian Coffee. Fresh fish and mix of Indonesian, Japanese, Italian cuisine.
