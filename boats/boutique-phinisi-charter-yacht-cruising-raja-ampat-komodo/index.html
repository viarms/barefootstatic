Boutique Phinisi Schooner Manta Mae

  • 30 m
  • 4 Cabins
  • 8 Crew

Launched in 2014, this gorgeous 30-meter boutique phinisi-style vessel truly captures the spirit of Indonesia and offers a unique opportunity to explore the archipelago in style and comfort. Her all-wooden hull and structure are based on the traditional Indonesian craft of ship building. The stunning boutique phinisi displays excellent taste along all lines. On the inside, mosaic panels made from coral, shells, fossilised woods, labradorite and quartz from Madagascar play in the light and transform your trip into a magical experience. She is the perfect choice for an extraordinary boat charter. 

Manta Mea

The spacious main deck on Manta Mea is perfect for sunbathing. It has a large and comfortable seating area with a beautiful view. When you are not busy diving, snorkeling or exploring the deck is the ideal area to relax and enjoy a fresh and delicious meal. The oval dining table is perfect for a family dinner or an evening gathering. The upper deck of the boutique phinisi provides even more space. Enjoy sun bathing, reading, an indulgent massage, or napping in the hammocks.

Cruising

Cruising the Indonesian Archipelago with Manta Mea is an adventure. The yacht is fully equipped with nitrox compressors and brand new Mares equipment for scuba diving and snorkeling. Moreover, sea kayaks, jet skis and underwater scooters are available on-board Manta Mae.The professional and friendly crew of the boutique phinisi will be there for your safety and provide the highest standard of service. They will do the utmost to make your trip unforgettable. Explore Komodo National Park and Raja Ampat with this exceptional vessel.

Cabins

The Manta Mae boutique phinisi has 3 cabins for up to 9 guests. The master suite features a king size bed and lacks no comfort. The other 2 cabins are equipped with queen size beds and offer plenty of space. All cabins have their unique style and en-suit bathrooms. Air conditioning, TVs and in-room safes complement the interior of the cabins. On request there are additional single beds available. Cruising on Manta Mea redefines travelling with style.

Manta Mae Specifications

Technical
  • Length 30,35 m 
  • Beam 6,67 m 
  • Draft  2,70 m 
  • Gross Tonnage 98 tons 
  • Engines Mitsubishi M/E type 8DC9, 295 HP 
  • Cruising Speed  7–9KNOTS 
  • Safety :2 * 10 persons 
  • Diesel Generators 
  • 2 * Mitsubishi 50 KW ~ 1 * Yanmar 10 KW + 1 * Emergency Generator Krisbow 5 KW 
  • Available Voltage 
  • 220 Volt Suko 
  • Water Tank 
  • 6,000 liter + osmosis system 
  • Fuel Tank 6.5 tons 
  • 2 Bauer Mariner, Nitrox membrane system available
2 Tenders

1 RIB tender 5.2 MT with outboard 100 HP engine

1 RIB tender 4 MT with 25HP engine

Dive & Leisure equipment
  • 8 sets Mares Dive Equipment
  • 4 Kayaks
  • 4 SeaDoo Sea scooter
  • Fishing Rods
  • Echo sounder
  • Nitrox (Bauer Compressor)
  • Wakeboard
  • waterskis
  • fishing equipment
Cabins

All cabins are are fully air-conditioned and feature ensuite bathrooms with hot water.

Interior

.The interior has unique mosaic panels created from materials found on the beach such as coral sand shells from Indonesia, combined with fossil woods, labradorite (the power stone of magic) and quartz imported from Madagascar.
