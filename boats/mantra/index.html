Mantra

  • 28 M
  • 3 CabinS
  • 5 Crew

The classic sailing yacht Mantra has been cruising Indonesian islands since 2015. Built in Sulawesi by using ancient boat building techniques this phinisi-style vessel will take you back to the days of spice trading. Fully equipped for a thrilling adventure, Mantara is an excellent choice for your private boat charter. Explore thriving corals, mangroves and towering volcanoes and enjoy breathtaking views. Mantra combines style and comfort in an unique way. The yacht features a kids friendly layout and is perfect for spending quality time with your loved ones. 

Cruising on Mantra

Get ready for an unforgettable cruise onboard Mantra. Her crew will spoil you with first-class service and a mouth-watering cuisine. A PADI certified dive master will take you on a tour through the breath-taking underwater world of Indonesia. Sounds beautiful, doesn't it? Mantra is available for all-year cruising across the eastern Indonesian waters. Explore Komodo National Park from April to September and Raja Ampat from November to March. Experience the seasonal crossing between South Sulawesi, Banda and the Moluccas and discover marine life unlike anywhere else. With a relocation fee, Mantra is also available for private charters to other destinations of your choice. Get in touch with our team and get your quote here.

Cabins

Mantra has 3 cabins. Each cabin features a queen-size bed and either one or two additional side beds. The additional beds increase the maximum number of guests to 10. All cabins are equipped with air-conditioning and en-suite facilities. The light teak panels and Indonesian traditional fabric creates a relaxing ambiance and ensure a comfortable rest after a day of adventure. On the main deck, an indoor lounge with a library and dining area is the perfect spot to enjoy quality time with your loved ones. There is an alfresco dining area on the aft deck and a large sun deck on the top deck. Enjoy delicious meals, cold drinks and warm sun rays on board Mantra.

Remote Islands

This beautiful adventure schooner takes you to remote islands. She features a low draught which is perfect to access the most beautiful anchorage and lagoons. The crew – real seaman from Sulawesi – consists of a captain, an engineer plus assistant, a chef and a dinghy driver, as well as an experienced cruise director. Moreover, instructors for Scuba Diving, Freediving and Yoga are available on request.

Mantra Specifications

Technical
Type : Phinisi
Year : 2013
Cruising Speed : 8 knots
Length : 23m (75ft)
Beam : 6.5m (21ft)
Draft : 3.1m (10ft)
Crew : 8
 
Cabins

Guests : up to 8
Cabins : 3 (1 quad, 2 doubles)
All cabins are fully air-conditioned with en-suite facility with queen-size bed and an extra bed on each cabin.

Tender

1x 6m fiberglass tender – 40 HP

Dining Options

Indoor dining room and Al fresco dining area and lounge are available. Dining menu can be customized according to your preference. Please inform us with your dietary requirements.

Saloon & Bar

Saloon are available indoor with a library and air-conditioning with a small bar that caters to your cocktails craving and free-flow fresh juices.

Dive & Leisure Toys
  • Snorkelling gear
  • Full dive equipment
  • Compressor (Air & Nitrox)
  • Stand-up paddle boards
  • Kayaks
  • Wi-fi when in range
Inclusions

Full board meal and snacks; Non-alcoholic beverages; Dive master and crew; Water sport equipment; Complimentary towels and toiletries; Airport transfer.

Exclusions: National park fees; Alcoholic beverages; Dive equipment; Nitrox: Dive, travel, and health insurance; Crew gratuities and personal expenses; Repositioning fee.
