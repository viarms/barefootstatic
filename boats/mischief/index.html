Mischief

  • 30M
  • 3 CabinS
  • 7 CreW

Launched in early 2016, the 30-metre Mischief luxury yacht was built by legendary Sulawesi shipbuilders and is an ode to the days of spice trade. With her contemporary beach cottage style, the vessel combines luxury and comfort in a perfect way. The charming tropical hardwood with natural finish and the light, neutral colours make her one of the finest Indonesian yachts. All 3 cabins are located above deck and feature large windows that allow for natural light. Mischief is the perfect choice for families and couples who want to explore and discover the beauty of Indonesia with comfort and style. Follow the footsteps of the ancient seafarers and start your journey onboard this traditional yacht.  

About Mischief

Unlike other wooden yachts in her class, Mischief displays a wonderful blend of light and neutral hues. This combination creates an airy and spacious atmosphere. The lounge and bar area welcome you with plush seating and elegant design. The upper deck with additional seats provides spectacular views and main deck offers plenty of space to relax and enjoy the mild breeze. The large dining area is situated on the aft deck and is the perfect spot to savor a delicious meal prepared by Mischief’s private chef. The crew members are experienced professionals and provide a world-class service that leaves no wish unfulfilled. Embark on a unforgettable journey and experience the pure joy of a private boat charter.

Cruising

Whether you are in the mood for diving, snorkelling or trekking, Mischief got you covered. The resident PADI certified divemaster will guide you through the unspoiled waters of Indonesia’s most diverse regions and the crew takes you on beautiful hikes in the rainforests. Enjoy panoramic views from top of the many hills and discover spectacular spots for your next family picture. Join Mischief in Raja Ampat from November to April and in Komodo National Park from May to October. Guests can also opt for adventures off the beaten track and explore the waters around Bali to Ambon. The island of the spice trade.

Mischief Cabins

The 3 cabins on Mischief are spacious and well thought out. They can accommodate up to 8 guests and are well located onboard the yacht. The master cabin is located on the upper deck and has access to a private balcony. The double cabin and twin cabin are on the main deck and also offer a stunning view through the large windows. The play of light and wood complements the design of the cabins in a wonderful and unique way. Each cabin is equipped with eco-friendly ensuite facility, power shower and walk-in wardrobes. Travel with comfort and luxury on board Mischief.

Mischief Yacht Specifications

Technical
  • Length : 30m (98ft)
  • Beam : 7.8m (25.5ft)
  • Draft : 3.3m (10.8ft)
  • Guests: up to 8
  • Cruising Speed: 8 – 12 knots
Leisure Equipment
  • 2 SUP
  • 3 Sea kayak
  • Water ski
  • Wakeboard
  • Sailing trimaran
  • Windsurfer
Dining and Lounge

Mischief offers a stylish bar and lounge area to socialize. The Al Fresco lounging and dining area at the aft of the yachts offers spectacular views over the surroundings.

Cabins

Mischief offers 3 Cabins:
1 Master cabin, 1 Double cabin and 1 Twin cabin. All Cabins on Mischief are en-suite and feature a eco-flush toilet, power shower and complimentary vanities. All cabins are  positioned above deck and boast large windows overviewing Indonesia’s spectacular landscapes.

Crew

Manned by a dedicated crew of seven, including a cruise director, a private chef and a PADI certified divemaster, Mischief will surely can offer a unique and customizable yacht charter experience.
