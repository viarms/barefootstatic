Tiare

  • 46 M
  • 6 CabinS
  • 17 Crew

Tiaré is a traditional yet elegant, 46-meter luxury yacht. Launched in 2016, she was designed by an Italian family and built by the proud craftsman of South Sulawesi. All 46 meters of Tiaré are dedicated to your comfort and well-being. She is a modern spin on the traditional Phinisi and made from tropical Ironwood. Her 6 luxury cabins can accommodate up to 12 guests and make her the perfect match for bigger families or larger groups of friends. The thoughtfully designed dive deck offers plenty of space for equipment and a hot shower. Exploring the Indonesian Archipelago is a unique experience and Tiare offers you a journey you will not forget.

Tiare Liveaboard

Tiare Liveaboard combines tradition and modern luxury in a unique and beautiful way. The layout and design evoke the feeling of home with a touch paradise. The light and warm wooden interior blends in perfectly and the well-placed lights generate a special atmosphere on board. The main deck features a salon with two dining tables and a separate library. Technical equipment, such as PCs and the indoor camera room can be used for editing pictures or videos of your latest diving adventure.

Tiare Cabins

The six cabins on Tiaré are your private retreat. Each of the en suite cabins is equipped with air conditioning, electrical outlets, and enough storage. Two cabins feature a king size bed, two cabins feature a king size bed and a single bed, and the other two cabins have twin beds. This sleeping arrangement makes it possible for Tiaré to host up to 14 guests and make her an ideal choice for bigger families and larger groups for friends.

Cruise on Tiare

A cruise on Tiarè is an adventure. Observe the world’s last living dragons in Komodo National Park and Alor, swim with the Manta Rays in Raja Ampat or discover the forgotten islands of the Banda Sea. Explore some of the greatest biodiversity in the world and get to know Indonesia in its most beautiful way. The 1st-class service of the crew and their dedication are the cherry on top.

Tiare' Specifications

Technical
  • Year build:2016
  • Guests:14
  • Cabins: 6 (2 king, 2 triples, 2 twins)
  • Crew:17
  • Cruising Speed: up to 10 knots
  • Length:46m (150ft)
  • Beam:9m (30ft)
  • Draft:3m (10ft)
Dive & Leisure equipment
  • Watertubing, SUPs & Sea Kayaks
  • Diving equipment for up to 14
  • Tender 1:Custom fiberglass tender 2 x 40 HP
  • Tender 2:Custom fiberglass tender 2 x 40 HP
  • Tender 3 – Rubber RIB with 15 HP
Dive Deck

Tiare owners are passionate about diving – so each diver to have his or her own personal space on board Tiaré yacht. Every diver has their own area to set up their equipment. Highly qualified staff is on hand to transfer your equipment to the waiting tender boats and after a dive will be  washed and dried by the crew.

Bar

A bar stocked with imported wines and spirits – a small cigar store is available for cigar connoisseurs.

Silent Generator

Silent Generators are located in the Engine Room – meaning 24h electricity with no noice – so you can feel and hear the natural surrounding without disturbance.

Comfortable Living Room

The living area on Tiare is designed to be a space where you feel totally at home with an extra touch of luxury.  If you need to rest, have a nap, read a book or watch a film – make yourself comfortable.

Thera are comfortable couches in the air-conditioned indoor lounge also has dining tables where dinner can be served as an alternative  option to the main deck depending on weather conditions and your preference.

Sustainable Attitude

Tiare has been built with sustainability a eco-friendliness in mind. Various small details, such as Eco friendly toilets, “NO PLASTIC” policy on board and the use of recycled wood where possible makes a difference to many other Yachts.
