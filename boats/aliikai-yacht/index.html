Aliikai Yacht

  • 40 m
  • 7 Cabins
  • 13 Crew

Aliikai is a spacious 40-meter wooden  Yacht built in South Sulawesi, the mecca of Indonesian boat crafting heritage. Fully renovated in 2021 she is ready to take you on an epic journey across Indonesia’s bountiful waters. Redesigned with impeccable taste and attention to detail, Aliikai hosts up to 15 Guests and flaunts 7 spacious cabins on 3 different decks. You can soak up the scenery from one of the several lounging areas or sundecks while basking in elegant leisure. Alikaii was designed with space, privacy, and comfort in mind. Aliikai Yacht’s size, comfort, lavish living spaces make the Yacht perfect for private charter  – weather it be for special events, wedding cruises or a family getaway.

Aliikai Voyage

A Voyage on Aliikai will bring you closer to Indonesia’s vibrant culture, history and mesmerizing landscape. On board, the vastly experienced crew of 12 will tend to your every need, and the Cruise Director will deliver a tailor made trip to remember. A gifted Chef will be onboard at all times to ensure each meal is a culinary experience rich with Indonesian fusion flavours. Aliikai offers modern facilities, first class equipment and a wide array of thrilling activities, to delight your adventurous side.

Cabins

Aliikai has 7 cabins in total, all fully air-conditioned, with ensuite bathrooms and closet space.The 2 Master cabins are located in the aft of the boat, each with its own private Seaview balcony. The upper deck has 2 double bed cabins with sea view. On the lower deck there are 2 double bed cabins and 1 double bed cabin with an extra sofa. In every cabin the detail shines through the exquisite interior décor. Upon request, the king sized beds in the Master cabins and upper deck seaview cabins can be changed for single beds.

The Yacht

Built with the strongest Ironwood from Sulawesi, Aliikai has 2 masts and 7 sails, totaling a sailing area of 450 sqm. With an array of inner and outer living spaces spread out over 3 levels you’ll be spoilt for choice for where to relax next. The dining area merges wonderfully with the main lounge and bar, which give way to the fabulous al fresco deck.Higher up you’ll find a 60 sqm sundeck for sun-bathing or stargazing, as well as the “zen”roof for yoga or simply to admire Indonesia’s stunning backdrop. Aliikai features cutting edge technology and sublime comfort to ensure an exceptional cruise to its Guests.

Aliikai Yacht Specification

Cabins

  • 2 master cabins with private balcony
  • 2 double bed cabins with sea view
  • 1 double bed cabin and single bed
  • 2 double bed cabins: can be changed into single beds

Living areas

  • Al fresco deck with dining area
  • Indoor dining area
  • Lounge & Bar
  • sundeck at the bow
  • Upper-level sun deck
  • A “zen” roof for Yoga/relaxation
  • Dive center & camera room

Cuisine

The talented chef creates dishes infused with the rich and exotic flavors of Indonesian spices. The menu has been designed to meet all guests needs, including vegan, vegetarian and kids-friendly options.For those interested in learning Indonesian Cuisine, you can join the chef on a market visit or get a private cooking class onboard.

Dive & Leisure Equipment

  • Snorkeling set
  • Nitrox
  • Diving Equipment
  • 2 compressors with double hose
    Nitrox
  • Diving center & Camera room
  • 12/15L tank
  • 2 Kayaks
  • 2 Standup Paddle
  • Beach games

Wakeboard

  • Tenders
  • 2 fiberglass dinghies with outboard
  • 85 HP Yamaha engine

Massage

There is an experienced and licensed therapist available onboard for massages on the deck or in your master cabin.

Build

  • In the Village of Bulukumba, South Sulawesi, Indonesia 2005
  • Being refitted in Serangan, Bali 2020

Technical

  • Length : 40 M
  • Beam : 8,5 M
  • Draft: 3,5 M
  • Engine:Nissan 450 HP
  • Cruising speed: 7-10 knots
  • Reach: 1000-1200 NM

Crew

Aliikai has a dedicated Indonesian crew of 12 led by an experienced western expedition leader.

Diving

Alikaii is fully equipped for Scuba Diving – there is ample deck space to prepare equipment and an air conditioned camera room for photographers and Nitrox is available.
